Hello,

Welcome to Checkpoint, my digital travel diary and creative project developed for my web development class. This blog began as a way to showcase the technical skills I’ve learned—HTML, CSS, JavaScript—but quickly evolved into something more personal. It’s a platform where I document the places I explore, the people I meet, and the stories that shape my perspective of the world. Each post is more than just a record of destinations—it's a reflection of growth, curiosity, and appreciation for the cultures, landscapes, and small moments that make travel so meaningful.

Checkpoint is also a space for connection. Whether you're a fellow traveler, a student learning to code, or someone who enjoys seeing the world through another person’s eyes, I hope this project invites you in. Through writing, visuals, and design, I aim to bring you along on my journey—not just across geography, but through personal discovery. My hope is that Checkpoint inspires you to seek new experiences, embrace unfamiliar paths, and find beauty in both the grand and the ordinary.
