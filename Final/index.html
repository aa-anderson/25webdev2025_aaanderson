Hello,

Welcome to Checkpoint—my digital travel diary and final project for my web development class. What started as a way to practice HTML, CSS, and JavaScript quickly turned into something more personal. This blog is where I share the places I’ve been, the people I’ve met, and the memories that have shaped how I see the world.

More than just a list of destinations, Checkpoint is about growth, curiosity, and finding meaning in everyday moments. Whether you're a traveler, a fellow student, or someone who just loves a good story, I hope this project makes you feel connected. My goal is to take you with me—not just on trips, but through the experiences that make those trips matter.
