Seoul, South Korea

August 2024

In August 2024, I spent a week studying abroad in South Korea. Though short, the experience was incredibly rich and transformative. From the moment I landed in Seoul, I was struck by the energy of the city—alive with a seamless blend of tradition and cutting-edge innovation. Our program focused on Korean culture, history, and technology, and I found myself learning as much outside the classroom as I did within it. Each day brought something new: visiting palaces, exploring local markets, and engaging in discussions with Korean students and professors.

One of the most memorable parts of the trip was our cultural immersion activities. We had the chance to try hanbok, make traditional rice cakes, and attend a K-pop dance workshop. These moments helped me understand Korean values in a way that no textbook could—through experience, laughter, and shared curiosity. The food was also a highlight. From tteokbokki at street stalls to traditional bibimbap served in stone bowls, every meal was a reminder of how deeply food reflects identity and history.

Even in just seven days, Korea left a deep impression on me. The program strengthened my interest in global education and opened my eyes to the nuances of cultural exchange. As I left Seoul, I felt more connected to the world and more inspired to keep exploring it. This trip wasn’t just a week abroad—it was a spark for future learning, friendships, and global thinking.
